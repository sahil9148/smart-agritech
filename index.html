HOW IT WORKS

Four Steps to Farmer Empowerment

From registration to better income — we support every stage of a farmer's journey.

01

Register & Verify

Create your farmer profile with land records and Aadhaar. Get verified in minutes to access all platform services.

02

Access Gov Schemes

Browse 200+ central and state government schemes. Apply directly through the platform — subsidies, PM-KISAN, Kisan Credit Card and more.

03

Insure & Equip

Get PMFBY crop insurance in minutes. Apply for equipment on easy EMI — tractors, harvesters, pumps, and more.

04

Sell & Earn More

List your produce on the marketplace. Connect with FPOs, mandis, and direct buyers. Get logistics support for timely delivery.
